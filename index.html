Our Story

Kan Zaman is more than just a BBQ restaurant; it's a culinary journey that brings the authentic flavors of Halal cuisine to the heart of Rotterdam. Our passion for great food and warm hospitality has made us a beloved local favorite.

We take pride in using only the finest 100% Halal ingredients, expertly prepared to create mouthwatering dishes that satisfy both your appetite and your soul.

Come and experience the Kan Zaman difference!
